Original Leo Lasagna

Description

I will share my recipe for the world's best Lasagne!

Please give a review of your impressions!

Ingredients

Basics

  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Balsamico or Vinegar

Sauce

  • 1kg Tomatoes of Choice
  • 1-2 Onios
  • 4-5 Cloves Garlic
  • 4-5 bay leaves

Meat

  • 500g minced Beef or a mix of beef and lamb

vegetables

  • 1 Paprika
  • 1 big or 2 small Onions
  • 2 spring onios
  • 0,5 succhine
  • 2 Carrots
  • add more vegetables of your choice!

Steps

Sauce

  1. Begin with the Sauce. Add 1-2 table Spoons of Oil in a hot frying Pan
  2. Meanwhile cut the onions and the garlic into small dices, the tomatoes into cuarters
  3. onces the pan is hot add the cutted onions and garlic and fry them till they are slightly golden
  4. Add a splash of the vinegar or the balsamico, a tablespoon of brown sugar and put the bay leaves into the pan and let them cook for 2 minutes
  5. Add the tomatoes and let it cook for at least 1h

Mass

  1. Add a tablespoon of olive oil in a hot frying pan
  2. Once hot put the Meat of your choice into the pan a let it fry for 2-3 min until nearly everything is cooked
  3. Add paprika polver, salt and black pepper
  4. Add the vegetables and let it cook for 5 min
  5. Put the sauce into the pan and mix it up
  6. Add one cup of Crme Fraiche and mix it up again
  7. Now prepare the lasagne begin with one layer of the Pasta and end with a layer of mass
  8. Add the Cheese of your choice and put everything for at least 30min into the oven
