Leo's Famous Mushroom chicken

Description

I will share my recipe for the world's best Muchroom Chicken!

Please give a review of your impressions!

Ingredients

Basics

  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper & Paprika Polver
  • Balsamico or Vinegar

Sauce

  • 400g Mushrooms
  • 1-2 Onios
  • 1-2 cups of heavy cream

Meat

  • 400g chicken breast
  • 2 medium sized eggs
  • 2 cup flour

Steps

Chicken

  1. Begin with the chicken. Cut the chicken breast into small chunks
  2. squirl up the egg and add some salt, pepper and paprika polver
  3. Put the flour into a big bowl. Add a layer of flour to the chicken chunks and then put them into the egg. Fish them out again and add another layer of flour
  4. Meanwhile put 1-2 tablespoons of olive oil in a hot frying pan. Once hot, add the flour coated chicken into the pan and let them fry until they are golden

Sauce

  1. Once all the chicken is done, but them aside and add the cutted onions and mushrooms to the pan
  2. Add some salt, pepper and paprika in polver
  3. one the juice of the vegetables is visible add the heavy cream and let it cook for 15min at medium/low heat
  4. Add the chicken and let it cook for another 5min
  5. Serve with potatoes, pasta or rice
